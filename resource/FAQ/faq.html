FAQ

What is WayGrow and how it Works ?


WayGrow is a go-to platform where you just have to SignUp and fill up some details (this shouldn’t take you more than 5 minutes). Once this is completed you’ll then have access to a dashboard with Jobs & Missions, where you’ll see TONS of opportunities specifically curated for you based on your WayGrow profile.

Where I can see my previous works and profile ?


WayGrow provides you a simple and powerful dashboard from where you can manage all your work and settings of your profile.

What Do I Get for Completing a Internship/Mission ?


You can earn rewards by completing missions! All rewards will be displayed in the mission and can be checked by the student before applying.

What is Missions ?


A Mission is a task or a set of tasks that students can perform for a company. The mission can be done using the Frapp mobile app or website.

I Completed a Mission, Where is my Reward ?


Rewards May take a processing time of upto 30 days from the date that your mission is checked or approved. The responsibility of rewarding the students lies with the company who issued the mission. In case you were not rewarded, first ensure that all your tasks were approved. If your reward is not processed within 30 days, please contact support.

Where i will monitor my Earnings


WayGrow dosen’t provide any facilaty related to Payments on its portal, All the treansections will be taken care by your HR team or company you are working for.
